About

A little about Cyber Company Solutions

Cyber Security Company

We believe that cybersecurity is essential to the success of any company.
Inspired by Sun Tzu's words, "Know the enemy and know yourself," our goal is to help companies improve their cybersecurity by providing them with the tools and knowledge needed to protect their systems and data against attacks cyber and systemic failures.
To achieve this, we offer a comprehensive consultancy program that includes:
- Strategic consultancy on the company's cybersecurity: We identify any vulnerabilities or risks that may be present in the company's systems.
- Cybersecurity training for company employees: We help create a cybersecurity culture in the company, empowering employees to identify and report cyber threats.
Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you improve your company's cybersecurity.

Services

About our services

  • Strategic Consultancy

    Consulting includes identifying threats and vulnerabilities, developing security controls, and improving a cybersecurity culture within the organization.

  • System Bugs

    Identifying systemic failures is a core component of our company's proactive approach. Using advanced risk assessment and vulnerability analysis methodologies, we thoroughly examine a company's digital infrastructure.

  • Personalization

    What makes Cyber Company Solution different is the customization of its solutions. Our company recognizes that each company is unique in its structure, operations and challenges. Therefore, services are adapted according to the specific needs of each client.

  • Training

    Recognizing that cybersecurity is a collective responsibility, Cyber Company Solution aims to provide comprehensive training for all employees, regardless of department.

  • CTF's

    CTF is a strategy that goes beyond traditional training approaches and simulates real environments and systems, challenging participants to identify and exploit vulnerabilities in simulated systems.

  • Community

    Cybersecurity is an integral part of organizational culture and by making CTF training and competitions part of everyday life, the awareness and responsibility of all employees in protecting the company's digital assets is strengthened.
